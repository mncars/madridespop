This summer #MadridIsPop

This summer the exhibitions Pop Art Myths in the Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum and Richard Hamilton in the Museo Reina Sofía will turn Madrid into a centre of Pop Art.

Please note: The exhibition Richard Hamilton will be closed on the 9th and 10th October.
